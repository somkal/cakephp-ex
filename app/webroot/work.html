Web Design

‘Right hand’ Website was designed and developed as a single page with simple scrolling to offer on- demand assistance on day to day needs for senior citizens.Technology used: MAMP (Macintosh, Apache, MySQL, and PHP) application server, HTML, CSS, jQuery, JsRender and Google Maps Location APIs.
